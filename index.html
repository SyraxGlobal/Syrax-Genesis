Overview

What Is Genesis Access?

Genesis Access is a single cohort. It will not be repeated. The participants selected in this round form the permanent foundation layer of the Syrax ecosystem — present before infrastructure, before users, before market awareness.

Selection is manual and based on alignment, contribution potential, and long-term orientation. Early cohort members gain positioning that is structurally unavailable to anyone who participates later.

Important: Genesis Access does not guarantee allocation and does not constitute a public offering.

Rationale

Why This Approach

Syrax is a zero-knowledge Layer 2 blockchain built on zkSync. It is not yet public. It is not yet open.

The infrastructure being built now will underpin the entire network. Participants who align at this stage are not joining a waitlist — they are shaping the conditions under which everyone else later participates.

  • Forming a permanent foundation cohort — this round does not repeat
  • Ensuring early participants are aligned, not opportunistic
  • Building infrastructure before the market becomes aware

When the network launches publicly, this cohort will have been inside it from the beginning. That cannot be bought later.
